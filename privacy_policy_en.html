Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 8, 2026 | Version: 1.0.4

Welcome to IPTV ID ("Application"). User privacy is important to us. This document explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use this Application.

GDPR Compliance: This application fully complies with the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are located in the European Union or EEA, we will request your consent before collecting data for personalized advertising.

1. Information We Collect

We strive to minimize data collection. The types of data that may be collected include:

  • Data you provide directly: such as playlists or M3U URLs you add to the Application, playlist names you save, and favorites (channels you mark as favorites). All these items are stored locally on your device unless you choose a synchronization feature (if available in the future).
  • Technical and usage data: non-personal information such as device type, OS version, app usage statistics (e.g., features used), and crash information that helps us fix bugs. This information is collected using analytics and crash reporting services mentioned below.
  • Advertising data: information that enables serving relevant ads (e.g., device Advertising ID). The Application displays ads through third-party ad networks. For users in the European Union/EEA, we use Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before collecting advertising data.
  • Publisher-Provided ID: We generate a unique ID (UUID) that does not contain any personal information to meet Google AdMob GDPR requirements.

2. Purpose of Data Use

We use data for the following purposes:

  • To operate and provide Application functions (playing channels, saving playlists/favorites locally, etc.).
  • To improve service quality, diagnose and fix technical issues (through crash reporting and analytics).
  • To serve relevant ads when the Application displays advertisements.
  • To comply with legal obligations or lawful requests from authorities when required.

3. Data Storage

Playlist data, preferences, and favorites are stored locally on your device using local storage (we do not automatically upload them to our servers). Technical and crash data may be sent to third-party services (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics, Firebase Analytics) as described in the Third Parties section below.

4. Third Parties

This Application uses the following third-party services for certain functions:

  • Firebase (Google) — for analytics and crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Messaging). These services may collect technical device and usage data. Please see Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Mobile Ads / AdMob — displays ads within the Application. We use Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to comply with GDPR and request user consent in the European Union before displaying personalized ads. AdMob privacy policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543.
  • AppLovin — ad mediation network for displaying ads. Privacy policy: https://www.applovin.com/privacy/.
  • Unity Ads — ad mediation network for displaying ads. Privacy policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • IronSource — ad mediation network for displaying ads. Privacy policy: https://developers.is.com/ironsource-mobile/air/ironsource-mobile-privacy-policy/.
  • Mintegral — ad mediation network for displaying ads. Privacy policy: https://www.mintegral.com/en/privacy/.
  • Media Kit — video player library for streaming IPTV content (HLS/M3U8/DASH). This library is only for playing content and does not send your playlist data to servers.
Important notes about GDPR:
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • All playlist and favorites data is stored locally on your device.
  • For users in the European Union/EEA, we request your consent via Google UMP before displaying personalized ads.
  • You can change your ad consent preferences at any time through Settings → Privacy in the app.
  • The Publisher-Provided ID we use is a random UUID and does not contain any personal information.

5. Required Permissions

Depending on the features you use, the Application may request permissions on your device, for example:

  • Internet Access — required for streaming content and loading ads.
  • Storage / File Access — if you choose to upload M3U files from your device, the Application will request file read access (depending on platform and OS version).

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to help protect your data. Please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you store playlists containing credentials or private links, consider the risks before adding them to the Application.

7. User Rights

You have full rights over your data:

  • Access and Manage Local Data: You can view, edit, and delete playlists, favorites, and settings at any time through the app.
  • GDPR Rights (for European Union/EEA users):
    • Right to know: You have the right to know what data is collected about you.
    • Right to access: You can request a copy of personal data we hold.
    • Right to rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
    • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
    • Right to restrict processing: You can request limitation of processing your data.
    • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
    • Right to object: You can object to certain data processing, including personalized advertising.
    • Right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time through Settings → Privacy in the app.
  • Ad Consent Management: You can change your ad consent preferences through the app Settings menu. For EU users, you can change your choices about personalized ads at any time.
  • Opt-Out from Personalized Ads:
    • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
    • iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

To exercise your GDPR rights or if you have questions, please contact us at the email address listed in the Contact section.

8. Children

This Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child under the applicable age, please contact us to request deletion.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. If there are material changes, we will display a notification in the app or update the effective date at the top of this document.
