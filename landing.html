About IPTV ID

IPTV ID is a professional media player application designed for streaming IPTV content. Built with Flutter and powered by media_kit, it offers a seamless streaming experience on both Android and iOS devices.

Our application is a media player only - we do not provide, host, or distribute any IPTV content. Users are fully responsible for the content sources they add to the application.

  • Free to use with optional ad-free mode
  • No registration required
  • Privacy-focused - your data stays on your device
  • Regular updates and improvements
  • Compatible with Android 5.0+ and iOS 12.0+
  • GDPR compliant for European users
